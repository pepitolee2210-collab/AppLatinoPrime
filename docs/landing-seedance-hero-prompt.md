# Seedance 2 hero video prompt

Producto: MiCaso Prime by UsaLatino Prime

Imagen base: `apps/web/src/assets/landing/micaso-hero-keyframe.png`

Videos integrados en la landing:

- Desktop: `apps/web/src/assets/landing/hero-video-desktop.mp4`
- Mobile: `apps/web/src/assets/landing/hero-video-mobile.mp4`

Duracion recomendada: 8 segundos en loop suave.

Formatos:

- Desktop hero: 1920x1080, 16:9.
- Desktop premium/master: 3840x2160, 16:9.
- Mobile vertical: 1080x1920, 9:16, recortando desde el centro-derecha para mantener el telefono y los bloques visibles.
- Social square: 1080x1080, 1:1, recortando el telefono y el halo modular.

Prompt:

Create an 8-second premium Apple-style product film for a SaaS legaltech product called "MiCaso Prime" by UsaLatino Prime. Start from the provided hero keyframe: a cinematic studio scene with a premium smartphone showing a migration control app, modular 3D blocks in lime green, sky blue, tangerine orange and lilac, translucent document cards, glass layers, official-source visual cues and dark graphite/clean white lighting.

Motion direction:

- Camera performs a slow dolly-in from left to right, subtle parallax depth, no hard cuts.
- The smartphone floats gently, rotating 4-6 degrees on the Y axis, then settles facing the viewer.
- Modular blocks orbit slowly around the phone and assemble into a protective halo.
- Transparent document cards move from the background toward the phone, then slide into a secure vault-like layer.
- Small light trails and glass reflections sweep across the scene.
- Background grid and translucent panels move at different speeds for layered depth.
- The left side must remain calm and readable for live HTML headline text; do not add baked headline text in the video.
- End frame should match the first frame closely enough to loop seamlessly.

Style:

Premium Apple product reveal, cinematic legaltech SaaS, refined, trustworthy, no people, no flags, no fake government seals, no readable agency logos, no watermark, no clutter. Use deep graphite, clean white, lime #C8FF00, sky blue #4DA3FF, tangerine #FF8A00 and lilac #B99BFF. Keep the product polished, serious and aspirational.

Negative prompt:

No human faces, no stock footage, no dramatic immigration imagery, no flags, no courtrooms, no police, no fake legal documents with readable private data, no fake government badges, no random text, no excessive particles, no shaky camera, no glitch effects, no purple gradient blobs.
